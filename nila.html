Project Nila

A sleeping mask that vibrates and lifts the curtain in front of your eye when your alarm goes off.

This project was developed as a part of the class Designing the Absurd at ITP.

The aim of this project was to develop a product for a hotel that had the elements of a smart invention and a minimalistic and clean design inspired by MUJI products. This project uses BLE communication to connect to your smartphone and upon pairing with your device you can use the dedicated app to set an alarm. Once the alarm goes off the mask vibrates and the curtain in front of your eyes lifts up to wake you up. For more documentation visit this link .

Technologies used:

  • Android Studio
  • Arduino
  • BLE
  • Fusion 360
  • 3D Printing
  • Laser cutting
  • C++
  • Kotlin

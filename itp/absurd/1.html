Designing the Absurd

Week 1

For our first project, we are supposed to make a phone add-on.

Some initial ideas we've had:

  • tripod that's connected to the phone
  • IR addon for your phone
  • any sensor addition to your phone
  • usb-c to RCA
  • breathalyzer sensor for your phone that calls emergency contact
  • Midi interface addon for your phone / touch capacitive sensors
  • Guitar pickup add on
  • An LED strip / a phone case or a display or something like that that only lights up when you receive notifications or calls from a certain contact A phone stand that lights up different-colored LEDs depending on the notification received (Morse code or binary).
  • An audio speaker that plays the note sequence that's received by a text message

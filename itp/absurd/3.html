Designing the Absurd

Week 3

We had a fun ideation session, and in my cards I got "Interactive" and "Wearable". I grabbed a water ring game, two springy wristbands, a flashlight, an hourglass and a bunch of other tools to mount these objects together.

AquaNova (Wearable Ring Game)

AquaNova is an innovative and exciting wearable water ring game designed to provide endless entertainment and fun while keeping you cool on hot summer days. This unique device combines the nostalgia of classic water ring toss games with modern technology and portability, making it the perfect accessory for outdoor adventures, beach trips, and poolside gatherings. It's offline and doesn't require any batteries or charging. It's also waterproof!

The TimeBeam

The TimeBeam Wristband is a versatile and interactive wearable device designed to combine the functionality of a flashlight with the aesthetic appeal of an hourglass, all conveniently attached to a comfortable wristband. This multipurpose accessory is perfect for various outdoor activities, camping trips, emergencies, and everyday adventures.

These were the two products I came up with that combined "Interactive" and "Wearable".

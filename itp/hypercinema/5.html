Week 4 - AI Tools

For me using AI tools is not the typical route I would take when being creative. But it was a nice change in the course of actions and it was definitely uncharted territory for me. Me and my groupmate decided to stick to supernatural images intertwined with natural elements. We wanted our images to be somewhat uncanny but also resembling the natural environment of our planet. We also wanted to have lots of humanoid type robots as servants to humanity. We were imagining a storyline that was the exact opposite of most sci fi which is AI taking over humanity. Rather we envisioned a future where AI creatures were servants and were planning to stand up and fight for an equal presence.

Considering the storyline, a horror theme would fit our piece. I digged through and found a long composition made by an AI, it sounded eerie, just what we were looking for. I did some editting and perhaps using some other video editing tools we can turn these images into a video. For these images we have used Dall-E and Midjourney.
